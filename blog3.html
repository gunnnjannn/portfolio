InnovateAI JAM:
From Brainstorming to Winning Solutions

The InnovateAI JAM, hosted by Manthan at STME - NMIMS Navi Mumbai, was more than a competition. It was a tough test of AI skills, problem-solving, and teamwork. My team, FemTech - Mihika, Saloni, Swayam, and me. We took on the challenge and pushed our creative and technical limits. In the end, we proudly earned the Second Runner-up position!

The Challenge: AI for Real-World Impact
The event was structured to help participants solve real-world problems with AI solutions. Five AI application categories—such as Cloning AI, Predictor AI, etc.—were given to us. Our task was to:

  • Choose one AI category (we chose Cloning AI).
  • Select an industry or topic (we focused on education).
  • Identify an issue in the chosen industry. Then, create a solution that uses our selected AI type.
  • Write our ideas on paper for an initial elimination round.
  • Create a 2-minute presentation (PPT) in just 30 minutes and pitch our concept to the judges.

This wasn't coding. It was about understanding the problem. Then, you had to craft a good solution and explain it in a clear manner, all under tight time limits.

From Ideas to Execution: The Pressure & The Process
The competition was tough. Seniors joined us, making it tough for first-year students who started college in mid-July. Yet, we remained composed and focused.

  • We brainstormed and found a key issue in education: AI could help here.
  • Each team member took on a specific role, ensuring efficiency in the short time frame.
  • We focused on real-world use. Our solution was practical and innovative.
  • As we refined our presentation, we thought about questions from the judges. We also prepared solid reasons for our AI approach.

Facing the Judges: The Moment of Truth
With no questioning round, our 2-minute pitch had to be compelling on its own. Every word, slide, and example needed to show our idea's potential. The instructions did not allow any follow-up explanations. Conciseness and clarity were key, and we ensured that our concept stood out.

Lessons Learned & Looking Ahead
This competition reinforced the importance of quick thinking, teamwork, and adaptability. Success in high-pressure situations isn't just about knowing AI. It's about making smart choices when there are limits. We were the top junior team. This showed that the right mindset and strategy can overcome any lack of experience. Getting the Second Runner-up spot was great, but the real win was the experience. I'm grateful for my amazing teammates and the organizers for creating this platform. I am excited for more challenges ahead!
